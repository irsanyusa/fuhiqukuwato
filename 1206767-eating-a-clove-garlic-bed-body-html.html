Therefore, the popular recipe for treatment, for example, bleeding gums with garlic is ambiguous and requires a professional doctor's assessment. If the red marks on the toothbrush are associated with a lack of vitamin C, regular chewing of fragrant cloves can be an excellent firming agent, but if the gums bleed another reason, garlic can hurt.
