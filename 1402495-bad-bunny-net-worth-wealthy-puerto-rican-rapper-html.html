Who is Bad Bunny?

Bad Bunny was born in Almirante Sur, Vega Baja, Puerto Rico on 10th March 1994. He is the son of Tito Martinez Ocasio and Lysaurie Ocasio Declet. His mother is a retired schoolteacher and his father was a truck driver.

Bunny has two younger brothers namely, Bernie Martínez Ocasio and Bysael Martínez Ocasio. What is Bad Bunny age? He is 27 years as of 2021.

Career

Bad Bunny studied at the University of Puerto Rico, while still working as a bagger in a supermarket. In 2016, he released a song called Diles on SoundCloud, an online audio platform.

The song was noticed by DJ Lucian, a music producer who signed him to a record label called Hear this Music.
