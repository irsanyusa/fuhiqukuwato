15 Daring Dresses That Went Down in History

 Posted on July 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  236 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Sometimes celebrities like to shock the public and be more memorable by picking an unusual color or bold outfit like Jennifer Lopez did with her green dress at the Grammys in 2000. But in addition to this famous outfit, there are others that came out on top with the hashtag, “daring.”

We at Bright Side rounded up 15 bold outfits that celebrities wore and would like to share them with you.

1. Princess Diana and her revealing silk slip floor-length dress for the Met Gala in 1996

2. Lizzy Gardiner and her credit card dress in 1995

3. Priyanka Chopra and her epic Met Gala dress that involved 2 assistants and 1 bus

4. Cardi B and her Aphrodite-stepping-out-of-a-shell dress for the 2019 Grammys

5. Madonna and her boy-toy outfit for the MTV Video Music Awards in 1984

6. Angelina Jolie and her revealing black vinyl dress

7. Cher and her sheer black Oscar dress from 1988

8. Gwyneth Paltrow and her scandalous Vanity Fair Oscar dress from 2002

9. Lara Flynn Boyle and her ballerina outfit in 2003

10. Halle Berry and her swoon-worthy dress for a James Bond movie

11. Barbra Streisand and her see-through dress at the Oscars in 1969

12. Anne Hathaway and a controversial Oscar dress from 2013

13. Kim Basinger and her Frankenstein-like dress with Cyrillic letters on her sleeve for the Oscars in 1990

14. Rihanna and her revealing dress in 2011

15. Zendaya and her outfit with a metallic breastplate from 2020

What dress did you like the most? What kind of dress would you wear on the red carpet?
