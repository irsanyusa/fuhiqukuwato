Fashion is a huge part of show business, and some celebs surely know how to use it to make a statement. Sometimes red carpet outfits stand out for their class and elegance, while other times they are daring, unexpected, and anything but traditional. No matter the style, most of these bold choices really take courage to be worn with confidence. And the more audacious and quirky they are, the more we love gossiping about them.
