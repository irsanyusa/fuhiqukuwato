Gisele Bndchen, 43, Shares How She Maintains Supermodel Skin With Just Water

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  345 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

“I feel better at 40 than I felt at 30,” the famous supermodel shares. As she turns 43, Gisele Bündchen maintains her beauty with simple yet effective practices. She believes less is more regarding skincare and opts for gentle cleansing with water. Her approach to beauty and life is a great reminder that caring for yourself with natural and mindful practices can lead to lasting beauty and happiness at any age.

Her skincare routine is simple.

The supermodel believes less is more when it comes to skincare. Although she can easily afford the most expensive beauty products, she believes the best thing you can do for your skin is wash it with water. “I wash my face with water,” she said. Bündchen admitted to using a good-quality exfoliant occasionally, but keeping her beauty ritual simple is the key to her glowing appearance.

She doesn’t wear a lot of makeup.

Applying makeup might make you feel like a different person, but the truth is that many ingredients found in makeup might damage your skin. Bündchen says she likes to keep her looks natural. “I’ve always felt better with less makeup. I just feel more like myself. With my face, because it’s angular, less is best for me,” she shared.

She cleanses her face with coconut oil.

You don’t have to spend much money to achieve flawless skin like Gisele Bündchen’s. Turns out, some of the products she uses can be found right in your pantry. “I always remove makeup with organic coconut oil (the same one I use for cooking) because it is, in my opinion, the easiest way to remove all traces of makeup. I put it all over my face, remove it with a cotton ball and then rinse it all off with lukewarm water. Then I intensely nourish my skin with a good moisturizer,” she said.

She watches what she eats.

Bündchen is a big believer in eating clean and using organic products. “If you eat a bunch of greasy food, it’s going be hard to have really good skin,” she explained. “It is very important to drink plenty of water and eat food that comes from the earth, like fruits and vegetables, rather than processed food. All of this is reflected directly on the skin,” she added.

Gisele Bündchen stands out among celebrity moms with her unique and unconventional approach to parenting. Her methods show apparent effectiveness, supported by undeniable results, and her approach also helped her overcome the common struggles many moms face.
