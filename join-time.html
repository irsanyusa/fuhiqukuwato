Benefits at TIME
Employees at TIME enjoy a wide variety of benefits, including generous paid time off, competitive health insurance coverage, TIME’s retirement savings plan, employee assistance programs and many other perks. Employees are also invited to participate in staff-led activities, such as a book club, virtual yoga, and virtual DJ happy hours, as well as company-supported remote wellness sessions and cooking classes, and exclusive virtual “fireside chats” with changemakers from around the globe.

Equity, Diversity and Inclusion at TIME
At TIME, we believe our people are our greatest strength. An inclusive and diverse team makes our content better, our products better, and our workplace better.

Equity, diversity and inclusion are top priorities in our recruiting and hiring, and committing to an anti-racist workplace is core to our culture. We focus on retaining and attracting amazing talent and developing a workforce that honors collaboration, respect and teamwork based on openness and trust, and that reflects the world we serve. Employee Resource Groups help nurture a culture at TIME that celebrates community among staff and educates allies. Our Mentorship Program encourages employees to expand their networks and develop professionally. TIME’s inaugural Diversity Council helps ensure that equity, diversity and inclusion run through every aspect of our organization to make TIME a welcoming, supportive and safe environment for all.

Supporting Our Team through the Pandemic
TIME is dedicated to the well-being of our workforce and our community. The health and safety of our people worldwide is always top of mind, including throughout the COVID-19 pandemic when our employees have been working from home. As we plan for a phased re-opening of our office spaces, we will continue to follow local developments and health safety guidelines in our various geographic locations. In the meantime, we continue to engage and interview candidates for opportunities primarily via video conference or by phone. We provide a flexible culture and welcome candidates to apply for roles outside of their current location as many of our positions can be based remotely.
