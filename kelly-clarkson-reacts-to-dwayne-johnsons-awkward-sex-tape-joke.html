Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson was recently a guest on The Kelly Clarkson Show, along with co-stars from his new movie Black Adam. At one point in the interview, Johnson made an awkward joke implying that he made a sex tape with his wife. It took Clarkson a second to react.

Kelly Clarkson Reacts to Dwayne Johnson’s Joke

Clarkson asked Johnson to share what he thought Black Adam’s favorite movie would be. The host made sure to tell him that he couldn’t say Black Adam. Johnson took a few seconds to think, before giving an awkward reply.

“I made a movie one time with my wife,” Johnson said. Clarkson seemed interested at first, and then he said, “No, never mind. Sorry.”

It was then that Clarkson realized Johnson was hinting that he made a sex tape with his wife Lauren Hashian. The audience and Johnson’s co-stars started laughing as Clarkson got up from her chair and walked around for a few seconds.

“The amount of time it took for me to get that,” Clarkson said, before adding, “That would probably be a good… Don’t brag!”

Clarkson, Johnson Pay Tribute to Loretta Lynn

Did you know that Dwayne Johnson can sing? He proved it during his time on Clarkson’s show this week, teaming up with the host to pay tribute to late country singer Loretta Lynn. The pair sang a cover of Lynn’s song “Don’t Come Home a Drinkin’.”

People may have heard Johnson sing as the voice of Maui in the 2016 Disney animated movie Moana, but they might be surprised to hear him take on a country song.

Fans in the comments of the video shared their enjoyment of the performance, with one commenter writing, “WOW!!! I never knew that Dwayne Johnson could sing that great. Of course Kelly can do it with anybody. What a great duet, Kelly Clarkson and Dwayne Johnson!!”
