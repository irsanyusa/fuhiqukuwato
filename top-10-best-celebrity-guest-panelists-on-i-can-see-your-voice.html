The singing game show I Can See Your Voice has been a hit with audiences since its 2020 premiere. Hosted by comedian Ken Jeong, the show features a panel of celebrity judges who help a contestant figure out which of 12 mystery singers can actually sing. Over the years, the show has featured a number of celebrity guest panelists, each with their own unique insights and skills. Here are the top 10 best celebrity guest panelists on the show.

Top 10 Best Celebrity Guest Panelists on I Can See Your Voice

10. Donny Osmond

The former teen idol Donny Osmond is a talented singer with a wealth of experience. He is also a great judge of talent, and he always has a good eye for the details.

9. Shaggy

Shaggy has been a nominee for seven Grammy Awards. He boasts two victories in the category of Best Reggae Album. With a discography encompassing 17 albums, he was the perfect choice as a guest on the panel.

8. Paula Abdul

The singer, dancer, and choreographer is a true icon of the music industry. She has a keen ear for talent, and she is always willing to give advice and encouragement to the contestants.

7. Joel McHale

The funnyman from The Soup is always quick with a joke, but he is also a sharp judge of talent. He is not afraid to call out the bad singers, and he always keeps the show entertaining.

6. Margaret Cho

The comedian, actress, and singer is known for her sharp wit and her ability to connect with audiences. She is a great addition to the panel, and she definitely added some laughs to the show.

5. Rachel Harris

Rachael Harris, the accomplished actress, had her turn as a guest panelist on the show. Her involvement added an extra layer of intrigue to the proceedings.

4. Ashanti

The distinguished singer Ashanti took part as a guest panelist, contributing her insights and charisma to the show. Ashanti, celebrated for her R&B and pop prowess, has garnered multiple awards, including eight Billboard Music Awards.

3. Jojo
